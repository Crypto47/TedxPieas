TEDxPIEAS 3.0 - 2018


x = independently organized TED event

Theme: Bridging Rifts

This event occurred on May 12, 2018 8:00am
6:00pm PKT (UTC +5hrs)
Islamabad, Pakistan

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized (subject to certain rules and regulations).

TEDxPIEAS 2.0 - 2017


x = independently organized TED event

Theme: Fine Lines

This event occurred on May 13, 2017 11:00am
3:00pm PKT (UTC +5hrs)
Islamabad, Pakistan

There seem to be very fine lines between the good and the bad. A lot of the problems today seem to be because of the collective moral, social, and mental dead-end that humanity seems to have reached.
This year, the theme for TEDxPIEAS is Fine Lines. It depicts exactness and accuracy, perfect comparisons and differences. It is simplicity, and it is multi-faceted depth. It can be defined in many ways and as many things, and hence, it depicts the versatility of the talks Team TEDxPIEAS is working hard to showcase.

TEDxPIEAS 1.0 - 2016


x = independently organized TED event

Theme: Challenging Tomorrow

This event occurred on April 30, 2016
10:00am - 3:00pm PKT (UTC +5hrs)
Islamabad, Pakistan

TEDxPIEAS is an initiative to encourage visionary and creative thought process in our local community. TEDxPIEAS aims to create awareness among the people and initiate thought provoking dialogue by welcoming an audience of Thinkers who want to be Doers.
Our theme ‘Challenging Tomorrow’ urges bright minds to construct and build a better future through realization of the power of one’s self. We must be able to rise to whatever challenge the future might hold; as a generation, as a nation, and as the human race.
